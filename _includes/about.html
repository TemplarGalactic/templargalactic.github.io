About Templar Crypto Systems

Templar Crpto Systems came about when we noticed the lack of open source controllers for the space sim market

For the most part the only comerical available controllers are HOTAS systems, heyboard/mouse, or game console controllers, we wanted something diffrent

This is what spawned the Flight System MK.1, a 25 button, 5 toggle switch controller, that when paired with a joystick allows for a unique flight experience.

The controller is currently in the protype stage, with a Kickstarter campagin comming in the next few weeks
